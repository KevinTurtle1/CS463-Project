Project

    CS451: Numerical Computation

    I attended this course during my Fall 2022 course, and we did multiple programs involving numercial analysis like B-spline and La Grange Form.

    Note: Most of my projects for this course was done through Repl. I would fork the projects then use the shell tab rather than the console tab.

    Run this command: "gcc {the c file} -lm -lX11", after that do "./a.out"

    Additional Information: To see the final result you would need to click on the black box on the right.

    La Grange

    • Implemented the Legrange polynomial form where the user will click as many dots from right to left.

    Natural Spline

    • Implemented the Natural Spline where the user will click as many dots (with the minimum of 3 dots) from right to left. Then, it will trace out the Natural Spline.

    B-Spline

    • Implemented the B-Spline form where the user can click at the minimum of 3 dots anywhere in the white screen. Then, it will trace out the B-Spline Curves.

    Newtons Form

    • For this project, it will use the Newton's Method. The user will need to click at the minimum of 3 dots from right to left. At the end, my program will trace as many cubic functions then the leftover dots will be traced with quadratic functions.
    • In addition, it will calculate the integral/area for the cubic and quadratic.
